How to use

Click two points to draw a rope anywhere, even outside the video frame. Click a rope to edit it, grab an endpoint to move it.

Pitch follows height, pan follows horizontal position. Esc cancels or deselects, Delete removes.

About Loom

Loom turns video into a musical instrument. Ropes stretched across the frame act as strings. Whenever something moves across a rope it plays a note. Pitch follows the rope's height on screen, stereo position follows its horizontal placement, and each rope carries its own scale, voice and dynamics. Built for static shots where small movements become composition.
